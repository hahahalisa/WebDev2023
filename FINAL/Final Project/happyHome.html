The Nook Shop is thrilled to announce the opening of our new subsidiary Happy Home. We specialize in building dream vacation homes on the beautiful Paradise Island. At Happy Home, we understand that everyone has their own unique preferences and interests when it comes to creating their perfect vacation home. That's why we work closely with each client to design and build a custom home that perfectly fits their needs and desires.


Happy Home's team of experts take pride in ensuring that every aspect of the vacation home is tailored to the client's interests, passions, and preferred interior design. We strive to make every detail of the home reflect the client's personality and taste. Whether it's a beachfront villa or a cozy cabin in the mountains, Happy Home is dedicated to making the dream vacation home a reality.


Happy Home is committed to providing our clients with exceptional service, and we take the time to listen to your needs and preferences to ensure that the final product exceeds your expectations. We use only the highest quality materials and the latest technology to build a home that is both beautiful and functional. With Happy Home, you can trust that your dream vacation home is in the hands of experienced professionals who are passionate about bringing your vision to life.
