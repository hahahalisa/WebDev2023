Exciting Community

Paradise Island is a community that offers premium amenities and facilities to enhance the quality of life of its residents and visitors. With its stunning natural beauty and welcoming community, Paradise Island is a destination that's sure to leave a lasting impression.

Happy Home

Take a tour of the vacation homes constructed on Paradise Island!

Hayo's Relaxing Reading House

Hayo's Relaxing Reading House boasts a warm and inviting interior design, featuring comfortable seating, soft lighting, and natural wood accents that create a cozy atmosphere perfect for unwinding with a good book.

Yang's Comfy Log Cabin

Yang's comfy log cabin is a charming retreat nestled in a picturesque forest, featuring a rustic and cozy interior design that seamlessly blends natural elements with modern comforts. It is the perfect place to unwind and reconnect with nature.

Mae's Oriental Resort

Mae's Oriental Resort is a luxurious getaway inspired by traditional Asian architecture and design, featuring ornate details. The resort offers a tranquil and immersive experience, allowing guests to indulge in relaxation and cultural exploration.

Poy's Dream Bamboo House

Poy's Dream Bamboo House is a stunning eco-friendly home that showcases the versatility and beauty of bamboo as a building material, featuring a unique and contemporary design that seamlessly integrates with its natural surroundings.

Daisy's Cozy Wonderland

Daisy's Cozy Wonderland house is a charming and festive retreat that exudes warmth and comfort, featuring a whimsical and rustic interior design with cozy blankets, fur rugs, and holiday-themed decorations.

Cattie's Art House

Cattie's Art House is a vibrant and playful space that combines modern art and design, featuring bold colors, quirky decor, and funky furniture that creates a fun and lively atmosphere. It offers a unique and creative living space.
